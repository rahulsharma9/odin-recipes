Chicken Wrap

Description

Healthy grilled chicken wraps are loaded with chicken, veggies and dressings. These tasty wraps come together in just a few minutes and make a great lunch or snack! These wraps are extremely easy to make and can be prepared ahead of time for work or meal prep.

Ingredients

  • Half Burrito Size Flour Tortilla
  • 1/2 breast half, bone and skin removed (blank)s boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon Italian dressing
  • 1 tablespoon diced tomatoes
  • 13 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • 1 tablespoon peeled and seeded cucumber, chopped
  • 3/8 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fat free sour cream mixed with: 1/6 teaspoon crushed garlic, 5/8 teaspoon onion powder, and 1/4 cup assorted greens

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in dressing 2 hours to overnight.
  2. Combine tomatoes, olives, cucumber, lemon juice, and 1/2 cup of the sour cream mixture. Reserve.
  3. Grill chicken, let cool, and cut into very thin strips.
  4. Spread each tortilla with 1 tablespoon sour cream mixture.
  5. Lay one quarter of chicken strips on tortilla, top with 1/2 cup of vegetable mixture and 1/2 cup of salad greens.
  6. Fold in sides of tortilla and roll up tightly
